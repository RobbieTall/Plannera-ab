State Environmental Planning Policy (Housing) 2021

Consolidated extract for internal testing.

Part 3 Development standards

This part sets out baseline development standards.

Clause 3.2 Development on certain land

Development consent must not be granted unless the consent authority is satisfied the land is identified as an urban release area.

In determining an application, the consent authority must consider infrastructure capacity.

Clause 3.3 Minimum subdivision lot size

The minimum lot size for subdivision under this Policy is 400 square metres unless specified otherwise in Schedule 2.

Lots that contain affordable rental housing may be reduced to 300 square metres.

Clause 3.4 Height of buildings

The height of buildings must not exceed the height shown for the land on the Height of Buildings Map.

Where no height is shown, the consent authority must be satisfied the building is compatible with the surrounding built form.

Part 4 Housing types

This part consolidates controls for secondary dwellings and other housing products.

Clause 4.1 Secondary dwellings

A secondary dwelling must be located on the same lot of land as the principal dwelling.

The total floor area of the secondary dwelling must not exceed 60 square metres.

Clause 4.2 Dual occupancies

Dual occupancies are permitted with consent in the R2 Low Density Residential zone.

On corner lots the secondary frontage must be designed to address the street.
