Environmental Planning and Assessment Act 1979

Part 1 Preliminary

Division 1 Introduction

Section 1.1 Name of Act

This Act is the Environmental Planning and Assessment Act 1979.

Section 1.4 Definitions

In this Act—

  • "consent authority" means the authority designated by the regulations.
  • "development application" has the meaning given by section 4.10.

Part 4 Development assessment

Division 1 Development requiring consent

Section 4.15 Evaluation

In determining a development application, a consent authority must take into consideration the following matters—

  1. the provisions of any environmental planning instrument.
  2. the likely impacts of the development, including environmental, social and economic impacts in the locality.

Division 2 Contributions and conditions

Section 4.17 Conditions of consent

A consent authority may impose conditions on a development consent.

Conditions must be reasonable having regard to the objects of this Act.

Schedule 1 Local planning panels

Clause 1 Functions

Local planning panels are to determine regionally significant development applications allocated to them.
