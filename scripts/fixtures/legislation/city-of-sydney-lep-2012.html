Sydney Local Environmental Plan 2012

Extract of key provisions for Plannera prototype.

Part 2 Permitted or prohibited development

This part outlines the zoning table.

Clause 2.1 Land use zones

The objectives of the RU5 Village zone are to provide for a range of community uses.

Development consent must not be granted for industries in the RU5 zone.

Clause 2.3 Zone objectives and Land Use Table

The consent authority must have regard to the objectives for development in the zone.

Part 4 Principal development standards

Provides standards for floor space ratio, height of buildings and design excellence.

Clause 4.3 Height of buildings

The height of a building on any land is not to exceed the maximum height shown for the land on the Height of Buildings Map.

The consent authority may consent to a building that exceeds that height if it is satisfied the proposal exhibits design excellence.

Clause 4.4 Floor space ratio

The floor space ratio on land is not to exceed the maximum shown for the land on the Floor Space Ratio Map.

Clause 4.6 Exceptions to development standards

The consent authority may grant development consent for development that contravenes a development standard.
