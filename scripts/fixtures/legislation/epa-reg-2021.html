Environmental Planning and Assessment Regulation 2021

Part 2 Development applications

Division 1 Lodgment

Clause 25 Application requirements

A development application must include a site plan, elevations and a statement of environmental effects.

Clause 26 Notification of applications

The consent authority must notify adjoining owners of a development application within 14 days of lodgment.

Part 4 Certificates

Clause 68 Complying development certificates

The certifier must not issue a complying development certificate unless satisfied that the proposal meets applicable development standards.

The certificate must specify the plans relied upon in granting the certificate.
